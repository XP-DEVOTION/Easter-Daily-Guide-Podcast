Hey friends, welcome to Day 24.

Yesterday we found peace,
and today Jesus is tackling doubt with Thomas.

Let’s dive in.

John 20:27  
“Then He said to Thomas, ‘Put your finger here; see My hands.
Reach out your hand and put it into My side.
Stop doubting and believe.’”

Straight to the point.
Jesus meets us right in our doubts.
He’s not intimidated by them.
He doesn’t shame us for wrestling.
He invites us closer.

So ask yourself:
What doubt can you bring to Him today?  
How can He turn your “maybe” into “yes”?
He’s got the scars to prove His love.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, help me believe even when I doubt.
Show me Your wounds and Your love.
Amen.

He’s real.

Today’s practice:  
Journal about a doubt
and ask Jesus to meet you right there.
Let honesty open the door to faith.

Tomorrow, He’s on the shore.
