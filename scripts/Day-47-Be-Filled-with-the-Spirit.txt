Welcome, everyone — it’s Day 47.

and today He’s filling us up.

Ephesians 5:18  
“Do not get drunk on wine… Instead, be filled with the Spirit.”

Time to overflow with life and joy.

Think about this:
Being filled with the Spirit is better than anything else.
It lifts you, steadies you, strengthens you, renews you.
Ask for a fresh filling today —
and watch how it spills over into your words, your attitude, your presence.
It’s contagious.

Let’s pray:  
Holy Spirit, fill me anew.
Let Your presence flow through me.
Overflow in my life.
Amen.

Fill me up.

Today’s practice:  
Sing a worship song.
Invite the Spirit to fill you as you worship —
open hands, open heart.
