Welcome, beloved — it’s Day 43.

Yesterday we chased truth with the Spirit,
and today we’re learning to wait on Him.

Let’s settle in.

Acts 1:4–5  
“Do not leave Jerusalem, but wait for the gift my Father promised…
You will be baptized with the Holy Spirit.”

A waiting game —
but one filled with promise.

Think about it:
the disciples waited for Pentecost.
It was close… but not yet.
Their waiting wasn’t empty —
it was preparation, anticipation, trust.

So ask yourself:
How is your heart waiting on God today?  
Waiting builds expectation.
It stretches faith.
It opens space for Him to move.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, teach me to wait.
Prepare my heart for Your Spirit.
Amen.

Patience brings blessings.

Today’s practice:  
Find a quiet spot.
Spend 10 minutes in silent prayer —
no agenda, no rush.
Just wait on the Spirit.

Tomorrow, we’ll see how He prays for us.

That’s Day 43.
