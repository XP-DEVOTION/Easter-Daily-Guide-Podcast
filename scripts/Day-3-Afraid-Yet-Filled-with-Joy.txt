Hey everyone, it’s Day 3.

We’ve been soaking in resurrection joy,
and today we’re talking about sharing it.

Let’s get into it.

Matthew 28:8  
“So the women hurried away from the tomb, afraid yet filled with joy,
and ran to tell His disciples.”

Can you picture them running?
Hearts racing, breath quickening —
their joy was contagious.
They couldn’t keep it in.

And it makes you think:
How can we share Christ’s joy with others today?  
Maybe it’s a kind word,
a moment of encouragement,
or simply living with hope that spills over.

That’s our challenge today:
Spread it around.

Let’s pray:  
Jesus, let my life reflect the joy of Your resurrection.
Use me to share Your love with others.
Amen.

Let’s live that out.

Today’s practice:  
Call or visit someone
and share a message of hope.

Tomorrow, we’ll see Jesus meeting us in our struggles —
so stay tuned.

That’s Day 3.
If this is sparking something in you, subscribe and pass it along.
And tell me below: How will you share joy today?
