Hey friends, welcome to Day 6.

We’ve been basking in resurrection joy,
and today it’s all about worship.

Let’s get started.

Matthew 28:9  
“Suddenly Jesus met them. ‘Greetings,’ He said.
They came to Him, clasped His feet, and worshiped Him.”

What a moment.
Meeting Jesus face‑to‑face leads straight to worship.
He’s beaten death — and that’s worth celebrating.
That’s worth falling at His feet.

So ask yourself:
How can you adore Him today?  
That’s where the joy deepens.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, I worship You for Your resurrection power.
You are worthy of all praise.
Amen.

He deserves it.
