Luke 24:5–6  
“Why do you look for the living among the dead?
He is not here; He has risen.”

I love how the angels just cut to the chase.
He’s alive.

Those words are a wake‑up call.
Jesus isn’t stuck in the past —
He’s living right now.

And that truth?
It breathes new life into us.

So ask yourself:
How can that resurrection reality renew your spirit today?  
Maybe it’s shifting your focus from what’s gone
to what’s alive in Him.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, thank You for conquering death.
Help me seek You — the living God — in all I do.
Amen.

Feel that renewal yet?

Today’s practice:  
Write a quick letter of gratitude to God for eternal life.
Let thankfulness stir joy in you.

Tomorrow, we’ll see how this joy starts spreading —
so don’t miss it.
