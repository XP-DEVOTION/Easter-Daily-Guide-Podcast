Hey friends, welcome to Day 40.

Yesterday we looked up,
and today we wrap up Ascension with joy.

Take a breath. Let’s dive in.

Luke 24:52–53  
“Then they worshiped Him and returned to Jerusalem with great joy.
And they stayed continually at the temple, praising God.”

A joyful send‑off.
They didn’t walk away sad —
they walked away praising.

Because His reign brings real joy.
He’s on the throne,
He’s alive,
He’s ruling,
and that changes everything.

So today, ask yourself:
How can you celebrate that?  
What’s one way you can let worship rise in you?

Let’s pray:  
Jesus, I worship You with joy.
For all You’ve done, let my life be a song of praise.
Amen.

Today’s practice:  
Take a moment — sing, pray, whisper thanks.
However you praise, just let it rise.
