John 20:29  
“Then Jesus told him, ‘Because you have seen Me, you have believed;
blessed are those who have not seen and yet have believed.’”

That’s us.
We’re blessed for trusting without proof —
for holding onto faith even when our eyes can’t confirm it.

It’s a quiet strength we carry,
a deep confidence rooted in His resurrection.

So ask yourself:
How does that truth boost your faith today?  
Let it steady you.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, bless me as I believe in You without seeing.
Deepen my faith in Your resurrection.
Amen.

Trust it.
