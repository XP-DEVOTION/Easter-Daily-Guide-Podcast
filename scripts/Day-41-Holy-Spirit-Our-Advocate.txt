Welcome, everyone — it’s Day 41.

We’ve been journeying through Jesus’ Ascension,
and now we’re turning toward the Holy Spirit  
as Pentecost draws near.

Let’s ease into this together.

John 14:16–17  
“I will ask the Father, and He will give you another Advocate
to help you and be with you forever — the Spirit of Truth.”

Our Helper.
Our Advocate.
Always there.

Jesus sends the Holy Spirit to guide us,
to steady us,
to fill us with truth and presence.

So pause for a moment and ask yourself:
How can you invite Him into your day?  
He’s right here — ready to support, comfort, and lead.

Let’s pray:  
Holy Spirit, come.
Be my Advocate and my guide.
Fill me with Your truth and Your presence.
Amen.

He’s with us.

Today’s practice:  
Pray specifically for the Holy Spirit
to fill you with wisdom for whatever’s ahead.
