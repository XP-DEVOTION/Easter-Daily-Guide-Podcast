John 20:19  
“On the evening of that first day of the week…
Jesus came and stood among them and said, ‘Peace be with you.’”

Right when they needed it.
Locked doors.
Scared hearts.
And then Jesus steps in with peace.

So ask yourself:
What fear can He calm for you today?  
His presence is the antidote.

Let’s pray:  
Risen Lord, speak peace into my fears.
Let me rest in Your presence.
Amen.

Peace is here.

Write down a fear
and pray for Christ’s peace over it.
Name it, surrender it, breathe.
