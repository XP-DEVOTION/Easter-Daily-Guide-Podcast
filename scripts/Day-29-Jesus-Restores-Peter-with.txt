Hey there, it’s Day 29.

We leaned into faith yesterday,
and today Jesus is restoring Peter.

Let’s get started.

John 21:15  
“When they had finished eating, Jesus said to Simon Peter,
‘Simon son of John, do you love Me more than these?’”

Straight to the heart.
Jesus restores Peter with love —
not shame, not lectures,
just a gentle invitation back into relationship.

And He restores us the same way.
Grace wrapped in a redo.

So ask yourself:
How is His love calling you to love Him more today?

Let’s pray:  
Jesus, I love You.
Restore me, and help me love You more each day.
Amen.

He’s rebuilding.

Today’s practice:  
Write a letter to Jesus
expressing your love, your gratitude, your desire to follow Him.

Tomorrow, He’s with us always.

That’s Day 29 done.
