Hey there, it’s Day 17.

Let’s get started.

1 Peter 1:3  
“In His great mercy, He has given us new birth into a living hope through the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead.”

Hope with a pulse.
Christ’s resurrection gives us a hope that breathes.
It’s not stale.
It’s active.
It moves.
It lifts.

So ask yourself:
How does that living hope lift you today?  
Rejoice in it.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, thank You for the living hope of new birth.
Fill me with Your mercy and grace.
Amen.

Cling to that.

and share it with a friend who needs encouragement.
