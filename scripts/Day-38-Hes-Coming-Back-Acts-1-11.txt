Welcome to Day 38, friends.

Yesterday we rested in Jesus as our High Priest,
and today we lift our eyes to His return.

Let’s take a deep breath and begin.

Acts 1:11  
“Men of Galilee, why do you stand here looking into the sky?
This same Jesus… will come back.”

He’s not done.
He’s coming back.

The Ascension wasn’t the end —
it was a promise.
He’s returning in glory and power,
and He’s coming for us.

So ask yourself:
How does that hope shape your life today?  
It’s a call to stay ready,
to stay joyful,
to live like He’s on His way.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, I look forward to Your return.
Help me live with hope and readiness.
You’re coming back, and I want to be found faithful.
Amen.

Today’s practice:  
Pray with that hope.
Ask for strength to live faithfully,
to stay focused,
to stay joyful.

Tomorrow, we’ll fix our minds on things above.

That’s Day 38.

and share it with someone who needs a reminder that He’s coming.
