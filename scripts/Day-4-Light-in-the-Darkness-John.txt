Welcome to Day 4, friends.

Yesterday we talked about sharing joy,
and today we’re reflecting on how Jesus meets us even in tough times.

Let’s jump in.

John 20:11–12  
“Now Mary stood outside the tomb crying. As she wept, she bent over to look into the tomb
and saw two angels in white, seated where Jesus’ body had been.”

Even in tears, something amazing was unfolding.
Mary is crying… but Jesus is near.
It’s a reminder that even in our sorrow,
He shows up with His presence.

So ask yourself:
How can you trust that He’s close today?  
That’s where joy sneaks in — even through tears.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, comfort me in my struggles
and fill me with the joy of Your resurrection.
Amen.

He’s got us. Always.

Today’s practice:  
Light a candle as a symbol of Christ’s light breaking through darkness.

Tomorrow, we’ll hear Him call Mary by name — so good.
