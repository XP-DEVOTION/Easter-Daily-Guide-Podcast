Hey there, it’s Day 5.

Yesterday we saw Jesus near in sorrow,
and today He’s getting personal.

Let’s dive into this sweet moment.

John 20:16  
“Jesus said to her, ‘Mary.’
She turned toward Him and cried out in Aramaic, ‘Rabboni!’ (which means Teacher).”

One word — her name — changed everything.
Jesus calls her by name,
and He calls us too.

He knows you.
Your quirks.
Your struggles.
Your heart.
Your story.

So ask yourself:
How does it hit you today that He knows you by name?  
It’s personal.
It’s intimate.
It’s love straight from Him.

Let’s pray:  
Risen Savior, thank You for knowing me
and calling me by name.
Let me hear Your voice today.
Amen.

Listen for Him.

Today’s practice:  
Write your name in a journal
and reflect on how Jesus knows and loves you — fully, deeply, personally.

Tomorrow, we’ll see Him meeting more people. Don’t miss it.

That’s Day 5 done.

And comment below: How does it feel to be known by Him?

See you tomorrow.
