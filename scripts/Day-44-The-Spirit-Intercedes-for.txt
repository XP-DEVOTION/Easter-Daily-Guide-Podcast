Peace to you — it’s Day 44.

Yesterday we waited on the Spirit,
and today we remember that He’s praying for us.

Let’s ease into this.

Romans 8:26  
“The Spirit helps us in our weakness…
The Spirit Himself intercedes for us.”

He’s carrying our prayers — always.

Think about that.
When we’re weak, He steps in.
When we don’t have the words, He fills the gap.
When we’re tired, confused, or overwhelmed,
the Spirit is praying on our behalf.

So ask yourself:
Where do you need Him to take over today?  
It’s okay to let go.
He’s strong where we aren’t.

Let’s pray:  
Holy Spirit, intercede for me in my weakness.
Pray through me.
Align me with God’s will.
Amen.

He’s got this.

Today’s practice:  
Find a quiet moment.
Pray silently.
Let the Spirit intercede —
no pressure, no performance, just presence.

Tomorrow, we’ll explore His fruit.
