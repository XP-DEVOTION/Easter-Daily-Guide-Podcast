Hey everyone, it’s Day 31.

We’ve seen the risen Christ appear,
and now we’re stepping into something new — the Ascension.
He’s preparing us just like He prepared them.

Take a breath. Let’s begin.

Acts 1:3  
“After His suffering, He presented Himself to them and gave many convincing proofs that He was alive.
He appeared to them over a period of forty days.”

Forty days — not just waiting,
but preparing.

Jesus didn’t leave suddenly.
He walked with them.
He taught them.
He strengthened their faith.
It was like boot camp —
but filled with love, purpose, and presence.

So here’s a quiet question for you:
How is He preparing you right now for what’s next?  
What strength is He building?
What faith is He deepening?

Let’s pray:  
Jesus, prepare my heart for Your mission.
Like You prepared Your disciples, I want to be ready.
Amen.

Today’s practice:  
Read Acts chapter 1 — no rush.
As you read, journal this:
How is He getting me ready?  
It might be something small,
or something deep.
Write it down.

Tomorrow, we talk about a blessing from above.

That’s Day 31 — you made it.
