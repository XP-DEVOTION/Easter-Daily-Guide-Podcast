Let’s get into it.

1 Corinthians 15:20  
“But Christ has indeed been raised from the dead,
the firstfruits of those who have fallen asleep.”

That’s big.
Christ’s rising is our preview —
the firstfruits, the guarantee, the down payment on eternal life.
What happened to Him is what’s coming for us.

That’s hope we can hold onto.
A future we can actually rejoice in.

So ask yourself:
How does that promise lift you up today?  
Let it steady your heart.

Let’s pray:  
Jesus, thank You for being the firstfruits of resurrection.
I look forward to eternal life with You.
Amen.

That’s our future.
