Welcome to Day 26, friends.

Yesterday we hunted for Jesus in the ordinary,
and today He’s cooking breakfast.

Let’s dive in.

John 21:12  
“Jesus said to them, ‘Come and have breakfast.’
None of the disciples dared ask Him, ‘Who are You?’
They knew it was the Lord.”

Casual and caring.
Jesus provides a simple meal with big love.
He meets needs — physical, emotional, spiritual —
right on the shore of our everyday lives.

So ask yourself:
How can you trust His provision today?  
Big or small, He’s got what you need.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, thank You for providing for me.
Help me trust in Your care and provision.
Amen.

He’s got us.

Today’s practice:  
Make a simple breakfast
and thank Jesus as you eat.
Let gratitude turn your meal into worship.

Tomorrow, more peace.

That’s Day 26.
