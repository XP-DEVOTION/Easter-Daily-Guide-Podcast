Welcome back to Day 10.

We’ve been sitting in resurrection joy, and today we wrap up this theme with a shift —
from sorrow to rejoicing.

Let’s dive in.

Psalm 30:5  
“Weeping may stay for the night, but rejoicing comes in the morning.”

Such a beautiful promise.
The resurrection flips the script.
Mourning turns to joy.
Whatever feels heavy today, Christ’s victory can light it up.

So ask yourself:
How can you let that joy break through?  
It’s a game changer.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, turn my sorrow into joy as I celebrate Your resurrection.
Be my light today.
Amen.

Today’s practice:  
Watch the sunrise.
Thank God for joy that comes after sorrow.
Let the morning remind you of His promise.

So exciting.
