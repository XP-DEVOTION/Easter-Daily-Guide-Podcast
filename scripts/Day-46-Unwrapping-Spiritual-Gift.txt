Greetings, friends — it’s Day 46.

Yesterday we saw the Spirit’s fruit growing in us,
and today He’s giving us gifts.

Let’s ease into this.

1 Corinthians 12:4  
“There are different kinds of gifts, but the same Spirit distributes them.”

Gift time — for each of us.

Think about this:
The Spirit equips us for His work.
He places gifts in us on purpose —
encouragement, serving, teaching, mercy, leadership, generosity…
all different, all needed.

So ask yourself:
What’s your gift?  
And how can you use it today?
Seek it out. Lean into it. Let Him lead.

Let’s pray:  
Holy Spirit, show me my gifts.
Guide me to use them for God’s glory.
Amen.

Time to unwrap them.

Today’s practice:  
Reflect on your gifts.
Think about how you can serve someone today —
big or small, seen or unseen.

Tomorrow, we’ll talk about being filled.

That’s Day 46.

Share this with someone who needs encouragement.
And drop a comment: What’s your gift?

See you tomorrow. Rest well.
