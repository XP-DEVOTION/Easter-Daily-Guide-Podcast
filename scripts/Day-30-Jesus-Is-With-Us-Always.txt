Matthew 28:19–20  
“Therefore go and make disciples of all nations…
And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.”

He’s sticking around.
Jesus is with us as we share His love —
not sometimes,
not occasionally,
always.

So ask yourself:
How can you trust that today?  
His presence is fuel for the mission He’s given us.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, thank You for being with me always.
Help me share Your love with others.
Amen.

He’s here.

Today’s practice:

Let Christ’s presence flow through you.
