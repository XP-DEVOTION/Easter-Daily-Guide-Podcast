Hey there, it’s Day 37.

Yesterday we surrendered,
and today we remember that Jesus is our High Priest.

Let’s settle in.

Hebrews 4:14  
“Since we have a great high priest who has ascended into heaven,
Jesus the Son of God, let us hold firmly to the faith we profess.”

He’s our High Priest in heaven right now.
Not distant.
Not passive.
Not just watching from above.

He’s interceding.
He’s caring.
He’s covering us.
He stands in the gap with compassion and strength.

So today, hold tight to your faith —
even when it’s hard,
especially when it’s hard.

Ask yourself:
How is that truth strengthening you today?

Let’s pray:  
Jesus, my great High Priest,
help me hold on to my faith in You
and to the hope You give.
Thank You for standing in the gap.
Amen.

Today’s reflection:  
Think back to a moment when your faith grew —
even if it came through something difficult.
Thank God for it.
He was there.
He still is.

Tomorrow, we talk about His return.
