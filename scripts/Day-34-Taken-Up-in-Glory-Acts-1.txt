Day 34

Welcome to Day 34, friends.

Yesterday we were powered up.
Today, Jesus ascends.
Let’s take this in slowly… deeply.

Acts 1:9  
“After He said this, He was taken up before their very eyes, and a cloud hid Him from their sight.”

Up He goes —
back to heaven,
back to glory.

He’s not gone.
He’s reigning right now.

So lift your eyes.
Let that vision shape your day.
When you know where He is, it changes how you walk here.
His glory leads us forward.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, I look to You in heaven — You reign above it all.
Let Your glory guide my steps here on earth.
Amen.

Today’s practice:  
Just look up.
Find the sky.
Breathe.
Pray.
Picture Christ ascending — let that image sink in.

Tomorrow we remember He’s still reigning.
That’s Day 34. You’re doing great.

If this journey is helping you, subscribe.
Share it with someone who’s watching the sky today.
And drop a comment: What was your sky prayer?

See you tomorrow.
