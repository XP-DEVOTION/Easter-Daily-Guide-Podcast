Peace to you — it’s Day 49.

and today the Spirit is coming with power.

Acts 2:3–4  
“They saw tongues of fire… and all were filled with the Holy Spirit.”

Fire.
Power.
Filling.

Think about this:
The Spirit brings power — real, holy, transforming power.
Ask for that in your life today.
Let Him work through you, strengthen you, embolden you.

It’s Pentecost Eve.
It’s electric.

Let’s pray:  
Holy Spirit, come with power.
Fill me.
Use me for Your glory.
Amen.

Bring it now.

Today’s practice:  
Find someone — a friend, a family member, anyone willing.
Pray together.
Ask for a fresh filling of the Spirit.

Tomorrow… we celebrate.
