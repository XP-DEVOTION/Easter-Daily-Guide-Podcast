Hey friends, welcome to Day 32.

Yesterday we saw Jesus preparing us.
Today, He’s blessing us — right before He goes up.
Let’s take a moment and dive in.

Luke 24:50  
“When He had led them out to the vicinity of Bethany, He lifted up His hands and blessed them.”

Hands up. Blessings down.

Jesus could have just ascended… but He didn’t.
He blessed them first.
And that blessing — it’s for you too.
It’s not just a moment.
It’s a guide, a gift, a promise.

So ask yourself:
How can His blessing shape your day right now?

Let’s pray:  
Lord, thank You for Your blessing.
As You ascended, You didn’t leave us empty.
Let that same blessing fill my life with purpose.
Amen.

Today’s practice:  
Pray for God’s blessing over someone else —
your family, your community, anyone on your heart.
Speak it out or write it down.
