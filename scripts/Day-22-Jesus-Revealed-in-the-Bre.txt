Welcome to Day 22, friends.

Yesterday Jesus walked with us,
and today He’s revealing Himself over a meal.

Let’s dive in.

Luke 24:30–31  
“When He was at the table with them, He took bread, gave thanks, broke it, and began to give it to them.
Then their eyes were opened, and they recognized Him.”

Bread and revelation.
He shows up in the breaking of bread —
in the Eucharist,
in shared meals,
in the simple, ordinary moments.

So ask yourself:
Where can you seek Him today?  
It’s often in the small things that He shines the brightest.

Let’s pray:  
Jesus, reveal Yourself to me in the breaking of bread.
Let me know You more deeply.
Amen.

Open those eyes.

Today’s practice:  
Share a meal with someone
and thank God for Christ’s presence at the table.

Tomorrow, He’s bringing peace.

That’s Day 22.
