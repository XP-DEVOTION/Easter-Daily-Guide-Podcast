Hey there, it’s Day 25.

We faced doubt yesterday,
and today Jesus is popping up in the everyday.

Let’s get started.

John 21:4  
“Early in the morning, Jesus stood on the shore,
but the disciples did not realize that it was Jesus.”

Sneaky, but sweet.
He shows up where we least expect Him —
in ordinary moments, quiet spaces, simple routines.

So ask yourself:
Can you spot Him today?  
Maybe in nature.
Maybe in a conversation.
Maybe in a moment of kindness.
Keep your eyes open.

Let’s pray:  
Jesus, help me recognize You in the everyday moments of my life.
Amen.

He’s there.

Today’s practice:  
Look for a small sign of Christ today —
a kind act, a gentle nudge, a moment of grace.

Tomorrow, it’s breakfast with Jesus.

That’s Day 25 done.
