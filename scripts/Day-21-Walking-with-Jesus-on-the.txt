Hey there, it’s Day 21.

We’ve wrapped New Life, and now we’re stepping into Christ’s appearances.
Jesus is showing up.

Let’s get started.

Luke 24:13–15  
“Now that same day, two of them were going to a village called Emmaus…
Jesus Himself came up and walked along with them.”

He’s on the move.
Jesus walks with us — even when we don’t see Him.
Even when we’re distracted.
Even when we’re discouraged.

So ask yourself:
Can you spot Him on your road today?  
He’s there, quietly keeping pace.
It’s comforting.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, open my eyes to see You walking with me,
just as You did with the disciples.
Amen.

He’s close.

Today’s practice:  
Take a walk and imagine Jesus beside you,
speaking to your heart, matching your steps.

Tomorrow, He’s breaking bread.
