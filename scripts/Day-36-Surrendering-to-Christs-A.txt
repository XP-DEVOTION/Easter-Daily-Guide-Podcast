Ephesians 1:20–21  
“He raised Christ from the dead and seated Him at His right hand… far above all rule and authority.”

Above it all.
Every power.
Every name.
He reigns.

His ascension isn’t just a moment — it’s a message:
He’s in charge over everything.

So here’s the invitation:
Where do you need to surrender today?  
Not out of fear… but out of freedom.
Letting go into His hands.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, You are above all.
I surrender my life to Your authority and Your love.
I hand it over.
Amen.

Today’s practice:  
Get practical.
Make a list — what are the areas you need to surrender?
Name them, one by one.
He’s ready to carry it with you.
