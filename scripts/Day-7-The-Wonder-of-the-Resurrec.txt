Hey everyone, it’s Day 7.

Yesterday we worshiped the risen Christ,
and today we’re joining Peter in some holy wonder.

Let’s dive in.

Luke 24:12  
“Peter, however, got up and ran to the tomb. Bending over, he saw the strips of linen lying by themselves,
and he went away, wondering to himself what had happened.”

I love his curiosity.
Peter’s scratching his head, full of awe.
The resurrection is a mystery that keeps us guessing —
in the best way.

So ask yourself:
How can that wonder grow your faith today?  
This is mind‑blowing stuff, and it’s meant to stir something deep in us.

Let’s pray:  
Father, fill me with wonder at the miracle of the resurrection.
Strengthen my faith in You.
Amen.

Keep that awe alive.

Today’s practice:  
Take a walk and marvel at new life in nature.
Let every sign of life remind you of Christ rising.
