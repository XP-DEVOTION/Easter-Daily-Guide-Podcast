Come along — it’s Day 48.

Yesterday we were filled with the Spirit,
and today Pentecost is almost here.

Let’s ease into this.

Acts 2:1–2  
“When the day of Pentecost came… a sound like a mighty wind came from heaven.”

It’s happening.
The Spirit’s power is near.

Take a moment.
That power is coming —
real, holy, unstoppable.
Get your heart ready.

So ask yourself:
What are you expecting from the Spirit?  
It’s big.
It’s close.
It’s for you.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, prepare my heart.
Fill me with expectation for Your Spirit at Pentecost.
Amen.

Let’s get ready.

Today’s practice:  
Grab a pen.
Draw a simple symbol —
wind or fire —
something that reminds you of the Spirit’s movement.

Tomorrow… He arrives.
