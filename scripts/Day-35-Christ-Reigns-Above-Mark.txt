Day 35

Hey everyone, it’s Day 35.

Yesterday we watched Him ascend.
Today, He’s seated in glory.

Let’s begin.

Mark 16:19  
“After the Lord Jesus had spoken to them, He was taken up into heaven and He sat at the right hand of God.”

King on the throne — right where He belongs.

Jesus isn’t distant.
He’s reigning.
And even more… He’s praying.
Interceding for you right now.
That’s real care.
That’s real power.

Can you trust that today?
Everything is under Him.

Let’s pause for prayer:  
Jesus, thank You for sitting at the Father’s right hand.
Thank You for interceding for me.
I trust Your reign. I rest in it.
Amen.

Today’s practice:

Put it on paper.
Let it go to Him.
