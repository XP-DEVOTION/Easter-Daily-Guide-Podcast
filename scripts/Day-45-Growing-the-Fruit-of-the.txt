Hi, beloved — it’s Day 45.

Yesterday the Spirit prayed for us,
and today He’s growing His fruit in us.

Let’s settle in.

Galatians 5:22–23  
“The fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, forbearance, kindness, goodness,
faithfulness, gentleness, and self‑control.”

What a beautiful list.

Take a moment.
The Spirit is planting these in you —
slowly, steadily, intentionally.
They’re not forced.
They’re grown.

So ask yourself:
How can you grow in one of these today?  
Maybe kindness.
Maybe peace.
Maybe patience.
Watch it bloom.
It’s His work in you.

Let’s pray:  
Holy Spirit, grow Your fruit in me.
Fill me with love, joy, and peace.
Help me shine.
Amen.

He’s at work.

Today’s practice:  
Pick one fruit of the Spirit.
Practice it intentionally —
show kindness, choose patience, offer gentleness.

Tomorrow, we’ll talk about His gifts.
