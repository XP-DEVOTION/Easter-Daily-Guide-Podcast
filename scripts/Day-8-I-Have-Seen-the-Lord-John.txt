Welcome to Day 8, friends.

Yesterday we wondered with Peter,
and today Mary takes the spotlight as the first to share the good news.

Let’s go.

John 20:18  
“Mary Magdalene went to the disciples with the news, ‘I have seen the Lord,’
and she told them that He had said these things to her.”

Bold move, Mary.
She’s the first to proclaim it:
Jesus is alive.

And that’s our call too.
The resurrection isn’t something we keep quiet —
it’s something we carry, something we share, something we live.

So ask yourself:
How can you share His victory today?  
Even a small word of hope can ripple far.

Let’s pray:  
Lord, give me boldness to share the good news of Your resurrection with others.
Amen.
