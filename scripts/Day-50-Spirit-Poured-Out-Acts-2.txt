Hi, beloved — it’s Day 50.

Pentecost is here.

Let’s celebrate.

Acts 2:17  
“In the last days, God says, I will pour out my Spirit on all people.”

It’s happening.
His Spirit is pouring out —
not just then, but now.
Not just on a few, but on all.

Take a moment.

His Spirit fills us, empowers us, renews us.

So ask yourself:
How is He moving in you today?  
Celebrate it.
This is a party for the church —
a holy celebration of God with us.

Let’s pray:  
Father, thank You for Your Spirit at Pentecost.
Work in me.
Fill me.
Amen.

Hallelujah.

Pray.
Worship.
Thank God for His Spirit poured out.
